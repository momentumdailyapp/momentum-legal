🔧 Troubleshooting

⚡ Habits failing to sync

  1. Verify you have a registered, active Premium subscription.
  2. Check your device Wi-Fi/LTE network.
  3. Navigate to the settings tab and pull down the list to trigger a manual sync check.
  4. Log out and re-authenticate to refresh your Cognito security keys.

🔄 Slow loading or screen freezes

  1. Force close and restart the mobile application.
  2. Check the App Store to ensure you have the latest code patch installed.
  3. Restart your iOS device.
  4. Contact support if analytics charts fail to render after these steps.
